## What Works Now

A single link from a high-authority, topically relevant site is worth more than 50 links from generic directories. The math has changed, and your strategy should too.

## The Numbers

In our experience, 5-8 high-quality links per month consistently outperform campaigns placing 30+ lower-quality links. The key metrics to watch are referring domain authority, topical relevance, and traffic to the linking page.

Quality link building is slower, more expensive per link, and significantly more effective at moving rankings and driving referral traffic.
